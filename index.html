Venus Studio Tangalle

Photography | Videography

A Storied Journey Through Time (1973-2023)

Established in the quaint coastal town of Tangalle in 1973, Venus Studio has evolved into a distinguished institution in the realm of visual storytelling. With an illustrious history spanning five decades, our studio has been at the forefront of capturing timeless moments through the lenses of photography, videography, and drone technology.

Join Us on This Journey

Whether you are looking for a timeless wedding album, a captivating event video, or aerial perspectives that take your breath away, Venus Studio Tangalle invites you to join us on this remarkable journey through time. Our commitment to preserving your memories with creativity and precision remains unwavering. Here's to the next chapter in the history of Venus Studio Tangalle – where every click tells a story, and every frame holds a memory.
