A Legacy of Capturing Timeless Moments Since 1973

Welcome to Venus Studio Tangalle, a distinguished hub for photography, videography, and drone videography. With a heritage dating back to 1973, we have been preserving precious moments with passion, creativity, and a commitment to excellence.

Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1973, Venus Studio Tangalle has been a trailblazer in the art of visual storytelling. Our legacy is built on a foundation of dedication to craft, innovation, and a deep understanding of the emotional significance behind every photograph and video.
